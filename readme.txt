== Effortless bot protection for WordPress. Set up in minutes, secure your site with just a few clicks! == 

An Internet Bot, commonly known as a web robot, spider, crawler, or simply a bot, is a software application designed to execute automated tasks online. While some bots can be beneficial, many—such as scrapers and botnets—can consume bandwidth, slow down your server, steal content, and exploit vulnerabilities to compromise your site.

The **Anti-Bot Plugin** introduces an innovative approach to **bot management** and **WordPress security**. By default, it blocks all bots except those from Google and Microsoft Bing. You can also create a **bot whitelist** of approved bots, ensuring that only those you trust can access your site. The plugin includes a pre-configured list of approximately 20 reputable bots from major social networks, including Google and Microsoft. With our advanced **learning mode**, you can test the plugin and manage this list—adding or removing bots—without risking the blocking of beneficial ones. Additionally, the plugin features a visitor log to help you oversee this process.
